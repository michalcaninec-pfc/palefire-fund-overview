04 ·Acquisition Criteria

What we look for

Our investment sweet spot. Please bring us deals that match these parameters.

Geography
CEE & DACH core

Core: Czechia, Slovakia, Poland, Germany, Austria, Switzerland. Selective across the rest of Europe: France, the UK, Benelux, Nordics and rest of CEE.

Ticket Size
EUR 15–80m equity

EV €20–120m (indicative, not hard limits). Plus acquisition financing for platform builds with bolt-on capacity.

EBITDA
EUR 4–25m

Established, healthy, growing companies. Profitable, with proven unit economics and scalable margin.

Stake
Majority, open to minority

Majority preferred, ideally close to 100%. Open to a significant minority in exceptional businesses where founders want to stay invested and keep building with us. Buyouts, MBO / MBI, founder partnerships, succession.

Filter
Quality first, sector second

Quality of business and growth potential are the primary filter: we focus on traditional manufacturing and high value-added services where we can apply our technological and strategic edge. AI lens: invest where AI is a tailwind, pass where it erodes the moat.

Target Traits
Pricing power. Recurring revenue.

Resilient demand. Strong management. Operating leverage and platform potential. Long-term, evergreen hold.

05 ·How We Partner

Active partnership with management

Our four operating principles for working with portfolio companies

1

Deep understanding first

We invest the time to understand product, market and customer before forming a view. Our decisions are grounded in data, not assumptions.

2

Constructive challenge

We ask the hard questions and surface blind spots: the role of a board partner that genuinely contributes to strategic decisions. We strive to add value and provide new perspectives.

3

Strategic active ownership

We help define the strategic agenda (product strategy, M&A, key hires) and entrust operational execution to a strong, autonomous management team.

4

More than capital

We accelerate growth through digital, AI and technology, international expansion (CEE → DACH/EU/global), M&A roll-ups and cross-portfolio synergies. Value-add where it genuinely accelerates the company.

06 ·Working with Advisors & Financing Partners

What we'd love to see on your shortlist

Where we engage quickly, and what helps us move within days

What We Pursue

Bring us these

  • Founder-led succession in CEE and DACH, plus selective situations across the rest of Europe (France, UK, Benelux, Nordics)
  • Established, healthy, growing companies with pricing power, recurring revenue and resilient demand
  • Profitable, growing businesses, ideally with double-digit growth or clear organic / international growth headroom, EUR 4m+ EBITDA, with a regional-leadership thesis
  • Carve-outs from corporates where speed and partnership matter more than headline price
  • Platform builds and bolt-ons: buy-and-build where compounding is the thesis
Where We Pass

To save you time

  • Pure rescues: restructuring, turnarounds, distressed; startups, pre-revenue / pre-profit
  • Sub-scale assets below our €15m equity-cheque floor (unless flagged as a bolt-on)
  • Capex-heavy businesses without growth or with weak cash conversion
  • Commodity production or services without pricing power or a defensible competitive edge
  • Businesses dependent on government contracts (B2G); single-customer concentration without pricing power
  • Real estate development, gambling; AI- or cheap-import-vulnerable industries without a defensible moat
